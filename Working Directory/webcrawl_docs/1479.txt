Student housing
Application process
Applications for winter entry to housing are now open
Eligibility criteria
Priority is given to undergraduate students under the age of 23. All applicants must be full-time Concordia students.
 
Graduate students
Students enrolled in Graduate Diploma, Masters or Ph.D. programmes may apply for housing. Graduate students may apply to live in the Jesuit Residence on the Loyola Campus or in one of the more limited spots in the Grey Nuns Residence on the Sir George Williams campus.
Continuing Education
Continuing Education students enrolled in Concordia's Intensive English Language Program that have been accepted into an undergraduate program may apply for housing.
Exchange students
Exchange student (4 months) applications will be considered after July 1st. Applications for winter entry (January 2018) open the second week of October 2017.
Second-year students
Second-year students or those who have previously lived in residence must contact Residence Life if they wish to apply. Decisions are made on a case by case basis.
How to apply
You must first be granted conditional or final acceptance by Concordia University to be able to apply for housing.
Required to apply for residence
1.  A Concordia  netname and password
2.  A valid credit card number (Visa, MasterCard or American Express and Interact on-line)
Visit your visit MyConcordia portal , Click on your Student Center and then scroll down to Admissions. On the left hand-side you will see in the menu Housing Application (eRez).
There is a non-refundable $100 application fee that can be payed with Visa, MasterCard, American Express, or Interact.
At any point during the application you can click on “Save & Exit”; your answers will be saved and the application can be completed later. When your application is complete, you will be sent an automatic confirmation email. This is not a guarantee of a room or room offer. Applicants who are extended room offers will receive them via the email address they provided in their application. 
Roommates:
If you wish share a double room with a specific friend you can create a 'roommate group' during the application process. That friend's full name and student ID number are required to complete this step.
Room assignments
When are offers sent out?:
If you have received an offer of promised housing as part of your Concordia acceptance letter, you have until May 15th to submit your application to retain your guarantee of a room offer. 
Offers will start going out to promised applicants by the middle of March.
We will send out room offers to applicants without promised housing only after May 15th. 
We send out offers throughout the summer, until all spaces are filled.
We offer rooms based on:
Order of application
Availability of room type requested
Any other relevant data 
Students who are offered a room:
Will be sent a room-offer email with a deadline to confirm via the two following steps:
Payment of a $800.00 room confirmation fee 
Purchase of the residence meal plan which is $4300 in total for 8-month residents and $2150 for 4-month exchange or winter session only residents.
Room offers include:
Building, room type, and cost
Deadline by which date payments must be made
Cancellation clause
*Note that the offer will only be for a room type at this point, not a specific room*
Cancelling a room offer after payments: 
The $700.00 of the room confirmation fee is not refundable under any circumstance. The $100 infrastructure upgrade fee component is refundable.
Full meal plan is refundable, minus a $100 administrative fee.
