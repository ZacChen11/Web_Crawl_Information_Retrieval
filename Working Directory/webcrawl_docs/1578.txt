Student housing
Jesuit Residence (JR)
Located on the Loyola campus, the JR is a co-ed* residence for 61 full-time graduate and undergraduate students
Facilities & furnishings
Centrally located kitchenettes with kettle, coffeemaker, toaster, and microwave oven
TV lounges, reading rooms, games room and laundry facilities with iron and ironing board  
Wheelchair access 
one adapted double size room located on the first floor for student with special needs
Single private rooms have double beds with storage drawers
Double private rooms have two single beds
Armchair, desk, lamp, office chair, mini fridge, and bookcase
Ceiling fans in each room
Rooms have internet** 
Linens, towels, and pillows are not provided
*co-ed means male and female students share common living areas.
**Residents are provided with an Ethernet cable to connect to the VoIP System of the university. Resident rooms do not have Wi-Fi.
Room types
Private single (en suite private bathroom/shower)
Double private  (shared, with en suite private bathroom/shower)
